Fun Facts about Spencer

  1. Founded in 1820 and named after Captain Spier Spencer, a War of 1812 hero.
  2. Home to the Tivoli Theatre, an iconic historic movie theater.
  3. Hosts the annual Owen County Fair, showcasing agricultural traditions.
  4. Surrounded by picturesque natural landscapes and parks.
